Can You Guess the Country Based on its Weather?

Based on the weather of a random city in the world, guess which country the city is located in!

How to Play:

You're going to be given various details regarding the current weather and time of a random city in the world. You will then be given a multiple choice question containing 5 different countries that this city could be located in. It is up to you to guess the correct country!
